Men's Dance Shoes



This is where it all started for me. I was fed up with cheap, uncomfrotable dance shoes. My dance shoes are similar to men's dress shoes but with a lighter weight construction. Soles are thinner than a street shoe and are glued on rather than sewn.

Like the men's dress shoe, my dance shoes include an absorbent veg tanned insole and veg tan liner.
