Bespoke Huarache Running Sandals


You can find various versions of this sandal on the web but I wanted to bring in some design and construction elements from the perspective of a shoemaker.

  • A footbed of vegatable tanned insole shoulder because of it's moisture absorbing characteristics and because it tends to mold to your feet, over time.
  • Front strap is anchored between the footbed and the thin Vibram bottom, so the strap attachement points cannot be worn away.
  • Latigo leather straps.
  • Total thickness is 5/8 inch. So there is pretty good protection but defintiley still lots of trail feel.

Send these to me with a check for $75.00 + $8.00 Shipping (if USA) and I'll make you up a pair!
